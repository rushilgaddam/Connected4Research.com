About Us

Our Mission

Connected was developed by Rushil Gaddam and Faisal Alsulaiman to help students begin research at The Ohio State University.

As students who have done research in the past and are super passionate about conducting research, we found it very challenging to find opportunities that met our initial interests. This website helps all students at The Ohio State University learn about professors, their research interests, and their labs, to find the research they are most interested in pursuing.

Features

In the 'Research Areas' section, all of the research areas from professors are displayed, and a user is able to click on one of those areas to learn more about it before they decide.

In the 'Search Professor' section, you can search for a professor, and that professor's research interests, personal website/lab, and email will be shown.
